About Us

What is Bump Bites?

Welcome to Bump Bites, a haven for expecting mothers and the brainchild of a passionate team committed to ensuring that the journey to motherhood is as delightful and satisfying as possible. Our story begins with a shared understanding of the unique and often unpredictable cravings that accompany pregnancy, and the desire to provide a tailored solution for the incredible women experiencing this miraculous chapter of life.

We've redefined the pregnancy snack experience with a thoughtful blend of indulgence and wellness. Our team is thrilled to introduce you to our Pregnancy Snack Craving Subscription Box, a carefully curated selection designed to cater to the sweet, salty, and savory cravings that accompany the beautiful journey of pregnancy.

We believe that pregnancy is a time of celebration, and our subscription boxes are designed to be a part of that celebration. Join us on this journey where every snack is a moment of joy, and every box is a testament to the beautiful balance between indulgence and well-being.

Our Mission

At Bump Bites, we understand that the desire for delicious snacks doesn't mean compromising on health. That's why our subscription boxes not only offer a delightful array of treats but also prioritize your well-being. We are proud to provide healthier snack alternatives, with a focus on vegan and gluten-free options. Each snack is thoughtfully chosen to ensure that you can savor every moment without sacrificing nutrition.

Our commitment to your health extends beyond just your cravings. For an extra touch of joy, consider adding our Baby Box to your subscription, a thoughtful assortment of items for the newborn, adding an extra layer of celebration to your pregnancy experience.

In each box, based on the subscription of your choice, you'll find a delightful mix of treats that cater to your specific cravings, with the added assurance that they are crafted with your well-being in mind. From wholesome vegan snacks to gluten-free delights, our goal is to make your snacking experience as enjoyable and nourishing as possible.

We look forward to delivering a blend of deliciousness and health to your doorstep.
